Transworld: Modus Operandi

The 10th video in the Transworld series sure packs a serious punch. With a layout the same as The Reason, Modus Operandi raises the bar for skateboard videos. Featuring Marc Johnson, Mike Carroll, Chany Jeanguenin, and Brian Anderson plus many more. Once again every trick was landed on beat to the music. I love watching this video because the music is so good and it can really hype you up to go skate. I'm not going to write much for this review because I spet so much time writing the review for THE REASON. Like any transworld video this is a must have and a must see. Marc Johnson = smooth, Chany= Excellent Varied Skater, Mike Carroll= technical lines, Brain Anderson = Go Big or Go Home!! I especially loved the Stress section . It kinda made me teary eyed. So go Check this out and I feel like skating now!
